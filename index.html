Visit Fortaleza

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant city that is the capital of Ceara.

Top three activities to do at Fortaleza

Attend a show or a concert

The Theatro José de Alencar is a great place to spend your night out.

Go to the beach for swim

The Praia Do Futuro is a great place to swim sit down and relax and play in the sand

Para Motor

A paramotor is a motorized paraglider. The pilot wears a small engine with a propeller and uses a paraglider wing to fly. It’s easy to use, portable, and popular for fun and adventure.
